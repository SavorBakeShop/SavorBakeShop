Stefany Prine is the passionate and talented owner of Savor Bake Shop. Residing in Hattiesburg, MS, with her loving husband Adam and son Gideon, Stefany has established herself as a cornerstone of the local baking community.

In 2020, Stefany turned her love for baking into a thriving business, founding Savor Bake Shop. Since its inception, her bakery has become synonymous with exquisite macarons, cakes, cupcakes, and cookies. Her exceptional creations have graced many festivals, weddings, birthdays, and holiday celebrations, leaving a lasting impression on both individuals and some of the largest businesses in Hattiesburg and Columbia.

Stefany's dedication to her craft and her community is evident in every meticulously crafted treat she produces. Her unwavering commitment to quality and creativity ensures that Savor Bake Shop continues to delight customers and bring joy to every occasion.
